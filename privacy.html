1. Introduction

Welcome to the portfolio of Aryan Panwar (https://aryanpanwar.in). This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and protect your information when you visit my website. 2. Information I Collect

I only collect information that is necessary for the professional operation of this portfolio:

  • Contact Information: Name and email address provided voluntarily via the contact form.
  • Usage Data: Analytics data such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited (collected via basic server logs or privacy-focused analytics).
  • Cookies: Small data files used to store your theme preference (dark/light mode).

3. How I Use Your Information

Any information collected is used solely to:

I do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties.

4. Data Security

I implement a variety of security measures (SSL encryption, secure headers) to maintain the safety of your personal information when you enter, submit, or access your personal information.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal data I hold about you, to request that I correct any inaccuracies, or to request that I delete your data. To exercise these rights, please contact me via the contact form on the homepage.

6. Contact Me

If there are any questions regarding this privacy policy, you may reach out directly via the contact form on the homepage.
